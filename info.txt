# 🌎 World Class

### `run_day()`

→ Runs one full simulation day.

### `advance_time()`

→ Switches between day/night and increments day counter.

### `register_npc()`

→ Adds NPCs into the world.

### `register_location()`

→ Adds locations into the world.

### `log_event()`

→ Records important events.

### `show_world_status()`

→ Displays current world state.

### `check_escape_condition()`

→ Checks if the mystery has been solved.

### `trigger_random_event()`

→ Generates random world events later.

# 🔥 The Function I Think Will Become The Heart Of Your Project

### `decide_action()`

Eventually this becomes:

```text id="fn1"
Observe World
↓
Evaluate Fear
↓
Evaluate Hunger
↓
Evaluate Trust
↓
Evaluate Monster Risk
↓
Choose Action
```

Almost everything in the simulation will flow through this one function.
